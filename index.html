Common QPe vs Shopify Questions

QPe and Shopify may seem similar but both have different business models. QPe distinguishes itself from Shopify with zero transaction fees, allowing users to utilize any payment gateway without penalty. Unlike Shopify, which charges up to 2% per transaction for using external gateways, QPe offers cost-effective plans with transparent pricing. QPe also provides dedicated relationship managers to assist in customizations without the need for hiring developers or agencies, ensuring personalized support and seamless scalability for businesses of all sizes. Additionally, QPe emphasizes user-friendly customization tools and robust features tailored to enhance eCommerce success without unnecessary expenses.
QPe offers a competitive pricing advantage over Shopify across various tiers. Starting at $18 per month for its basic plan, QPe is significantly more affordable than Shopify's starting price of $29 per month. As you scale up, QPe's Pro Plus plan costs approximately $1250 per month, offering features like a dedicated success manager, which eliminates the need to hire additional personnel for managing your eCommerce operations. In contrast, Shopify's top-tier Shopify Plus plan costs $2300 per month, making it considerably more expensive than QPe's Pro Plus plan. Shopify's reliance on third-party apps, paid extensions, and plugins to fill gaps in native functionality can substantially increase the total cost of ownership. Each additional app or theme comes with an extra cost, significantly impacting your budget as you scale and add necessary functionalities to your online store. Therefore, while QPe provides cost-effective plans with inclusive features and support like dedicated success managers, Shopify's pricing structure and reliance on paid add-ons can lead to higher overall costs, especially for businesses requiring extensive customization and scalability in their eCommerce operations.
Migrating from Shopify to QPe is straightforward with QPe's Shopify import feature. Simply use this tool to seamlessly transfer your Shopify products directly to your QPe store. This process ensures a smooth transition, allowing you to maintain product data and settings while taking advantage of QPe's robust eCommerce platform.
Both platform offers 24/7 email, chat and phone support. Moreover QPe provides dedicated relationship manager and ensures 100% customer satisfaction.
